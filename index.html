Our Story

Where Austria Meets
the Red Sea

A hidden gem bringing the soul of Styria to Hurghada

A Hidden Gem

Heart & Soul in Every Bite

Heart of Styria — formerly known as Mr. Hans — is Hurghada's most beloved Austrian bakery and restaurant. We bring the soulful flavours of Styria, Austria's greenest heartland, to the sun-drenched shores of the Egyptian Red Sea.

For too long, Hurghada lacked a place where you could sit down to a proper Wiener Frühstück, enjoy a hand-rolled croissant, or treat yourself to a warm Apfelstrudel. We changed that — and we haven't looked back since.

Our Philosophy

Baked Fresh Daily

Every bread, pastry, and Mehlspeise is crafted from scratch each morning. No shortcuts. No frozen shortcuts. Just flour, butter, patience and tradition.

In a town where European restaurant prices have skyrocketed, we made a different choice — outstanding quality at fair prices that everyone can enjoy, whether you're on holiday or call Hurghada home.
