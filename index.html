Our Story

Where the Alps meet the Red Sea — a journey of love, bread, and culture

Where Two Worlds Meet

Heart of Styria was born from a simple but powerful idea: to bring the warmth of Austrian baking tradition to the sun-drenched shores of Hurghada. Styria — a lush, green region in the heart of Austria — is known for its rolling hills, pumpkin seed oil, and deeply rooted food culture. It is from this place of warmth and craftsmanship that we draw our inspiration.

"Freshly baked with love and care, the taste of home is waiting here."

Hurghada — Egypt's beloved Red Sea resort city — welcomed us with open arms. A place where European tourists come to relax, dive, and escape the cold winters of the north. Many of them longed for a familiar taste, a morning roll, a proper cup of coffee, a schnitzel that reminded them of home. We answered that call.

The Heart of Styria

Styria is Austria's second-largest state, nestled between the Alps and the gentle hills of the south. It is a land of farmers' markets, forest walks, and Heuriger — the beloved wine taverns where locals gather to eat, drink and share stories. The baking tradition here runs deep: sourdough breads, hearty rolls, sweet pastries for celebrations.

The Viennese coffee house culture — declared UNESCO Intangible Cultural Heritage — has spread its influence throughout Austria. A proper Wiener Kaffee, a slice of cake, and unhurried time: this is the Austrian way. We carry all of this with us across the Mediterranean, through the Suez, and to the shores of the Red Sea.

"Austria gave us the recipes. Hurghada gives us the sunshine. Together, we make Heart of Styria."

Hurghada — Our Home

Hurghada is one of Egypt's most vibrant resort cities, stretching along 40 kilometres of the Red Sea coast. Known for world-class diving, crystal-clear waters and year-round sunshine, it attracts millions of visitors from across Europe and beyond. For many Austrian, German and Swiss tourists, Hurghada is the go-to winter escape.

We are located in the Touristic Villages area — the heart of Hurghada's vibrant hospitality district. Our doors are open every day, ready to welcome you with the smell of fresh bread, the warmth of good coffee, and a menu that bridges two cultures.

"Between the mountains of Styria and the waters of the Red Sea, there is Heart of Styria — always open, always warm."
